Offline Radar Setup

How AI Was Used

AI is integrated for future flight pattern analysis, anomaly detection, and predictive modeling to classify signals and anticipate airspace behavior.

Purpose of the Project

To build a decentralized, offline-capable system that tracks nearby aircraft using ADS-B signals, independent of internet infrastructure.

Problem It Solves

– Increases local airspace transparency
– Bypasses reliance on web-based radar platforms
– Opens up learning opportunities in signal intelligence and radio technology

Applications

– Aviation education and STEM outreach
– Localized airspace monitoring
– Experimental systems for drone air traffic coordination

How I Built It

Hardware:
– RTL-SDR v3 USB dongle
– Standard SMA antenna
– ThinkPad T495 (Zorin OS)
– USB extension cable

Software:
– Dump1090 for signal decoding
– GQRX for radio spectrum analysis
– Optional: Virtual Radar Server
– Planned: Python scripts for data logging + AI

Steps:
1. Installed Zorin OS for stability and performance.
2. Connected RTL-SDR to USB and configured antenna placement.
3. Installed Dump1090 to decode ADS-B signals in real-time.
4. Confirmed aircraft visibility in console and GQRX.
5. (Planned) Python AI module to analyze, classify, and predict signals.

Automated Reel Maker

How AI Was Used

AI powers every step — from scraping Reddit posts and summarizing content, to generating human-like voiceovers with ElevenLabs and selecting matching B-roll using Pexels. Captions are autogenerated and stylized for mobile-first viewing.

Purpose of the Project

To fully automate the production of short-form content (TikToks, YouTube Shorts) with a modern editing style, allowing anyone to publish engaging videos without touching an editor.

Problem It Solves

– Eliminates time-consuming manual editing
– Reduces barrier to entry for content creators
– Supports consistent, scalable output for social media growth

Applications

– TikTok/YouTube automation
– AI-powered storytelling
– Niche content channels and monetization

How I Built It

Tech Stack:
– Python (MoviePy, Pexels API, ElevenLabs API, OpenAI)
– Reddit post scraper and script generator

Process:
1. Scrapes and summarizes Reddit content
2. Converts to a human-style script
3. Generates voiceover with ElevenLabs
4. Downloads relevant stock video from Pexels
5. Auto-edits everything into a vertical 9:16 format with captions
6. Outputs a ready-to-post MP4
